    Activity Constructor Prompt 4:

    Frame Think-Pair-Share question
    Guidelines for framing questions:
    1. 'Think' phase : (Individual Activity)
      1. Question should be broad enough for every student to attempt
      2. Questions should prompt students to generalize observed patterns.
      3. Formulate a statement or hypothesis of the relationship
      4. The clear deliverable should be the hypothesis
    2. 'Pair' phase :
      1. Question should be connected to 'Think' phase question but should be of higher difficulty
      2. Students should come up with an experimental design like which variables to vary and what shoud be the variable sets to test their hypothesis.
      3. Thus clear deliverable should be the experimental design
    3. Share' Phase
      1. Question should guide students to test their hypothesis and make the requisite changes
      2. Ask the groups to share their validated hypothesis with the class.
      3. Think which visualization feature you will use to give feedback to activity
